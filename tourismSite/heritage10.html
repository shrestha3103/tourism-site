Shaheed Minar

Shaheed Minar, formerly known as Ochterlony Monument, is an iconic structure built in 1828 in honor of Major-General Sir David Ochterlony's victory in the Anglo-Nepalese War. The monument was later rededicated to the martyrs of the Indian freedom struggle. Standing tall at 48 meters, it provides a panoramic view of the city and is a reminder of Kolkata's rich history.
