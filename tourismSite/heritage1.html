Victoria Memorial

The Victoria Memorial is an iconic monument in Kolkata, built in memory of Queen Victoria.
Constructed between 1906 and 1921, this grand structure is a blend of British and Mughal architectural styles, surrounded by lush gardens.
It serves as a museum containing memories of Netaji Subhas Chandra Bose showcasing the history of the British Raj in India.
