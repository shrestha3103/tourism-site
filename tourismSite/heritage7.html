Jorasanko Thakur Bari

Jorasanko Thakur Bari, also known as the House of the Thakurs, is the ancestral home of the Tagore family. It is located in the Jorasanko neighborhood of Kolkata. The house is famous for being the birthplace of the Nobel laureate Rabindranath Tagore, one of the most influential figures in Indian literature and culture.
Today, Jorasanko Thakur Bari is a museum dedicated to the life and works of Rabindranath Tagore, offering visitors a glimpse into the world of one of India's greatest poets and thinkers. The building itself is an architectural marvel with its colonial-era design and lush gardens.
